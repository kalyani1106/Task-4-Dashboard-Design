# Task 4 - Dashboard Design

## Objective

Design an interactive dashboard for business stakeholders using Power BI to monitor sales performance, profitability, and customer segments.

## Tool Used

* Power BI Desktop

## Dataset

* Sample Superstore Dataset

## Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards (Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Quantity, Profit Margin)
* Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
* Sales by Category
* Profit by Region
* Sales by Segment
* Interactive Slicer Filters
* Consistent Dashboard Theme

## Key Insights

* Total Sales reached $2.30M.
* Total Profit generated was $286.40K.
* Overall Profit Margin is 12.47%.
* Technology category generated the highest sales ($0.84M).
* West region generated the highest profit ($108K).
* Consumer segment contributed 50.56% of total sales.
* Sales peaked during November and December.

## Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on Technology products.
* Expand successful sales strategies in the West region.
* Increase engagement with Consumer segment customers.
* Monitor seasonal peaks to maximize year-end revenue.

## Deliverables

* Interactive Power BI Dashboard (.pbix)
* Dashboard Screenshot
* PPT Summary
* Dataset

## Outcome

Developed an interactive business dashboard that enables stakeholders to track key performance indicators, identify sales trends, analyze profitability, and make data-driven decisions.
